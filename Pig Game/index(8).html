How to play Pig Game

GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT score. - BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his CURRENT score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn. - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn. - The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
